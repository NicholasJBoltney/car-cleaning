## MOBILE-SPECIFIC CONSIDERATIONS

**Above the fold (mobile):**
- Headline (slightly shorter)
- Subheadline
- Hero image (portrait orientation optimized)
- One large CTA button
- Small trust badge

**Throughout page:**
- Single column layout
- Larger tap targets (buttons minimum 44px height)
- Click-to-call phone number in header
- Simplified navigation (hamburger menu)
- Gallery: Swipe carousel instead of grid
- Testimonials: Carousel format
- FAQ: Accordion format to save space

## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Deep Black (#0A0A0A) or Charcoal (#2C2C2C)
- Secondary: Pure White (#FFFFFF)
- Accent 1: Metallic Silver (#C0C0C0) or Gold (#D4AF37)
- Accent 2: Deep Blue (#1A365D) or Burgundy (#6B1C23)
- Background variations: Off-white (#F7F7F7), Light Gray (#E5E5E5)

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Montserrat Bold or Playfair Display (serif)
- Body: Inter, Helvetica Neue, or SF Pro
- Sizes: H1 (48-60px), H2 (36-42px), Body (16-18px)
- Line spacing: 1.5-1.6 for readability

**Button Styling:**
- Primary CTA: Accent color, white text, subtle shadow
- Hover state: Slight brightness increase or scale
- Minimum size: 48px height (mobile), larger on desktop
- Border radius: 4-8px (modern but not overly rounded)

**Imagery:**
- All photos: High resolution (at least 1920px wide for hero)
- Consistent lighting and color grading
- Professional editing (no phone snapshots)
- Optimized for web (compressed but high quality)

**Spacing:**
- Section padding: 80-120px top/bottom (desktop)
- Element margins: 24-48px between major elements
- Generous white space‚Äînever cramped
